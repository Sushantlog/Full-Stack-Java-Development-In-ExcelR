ndian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) is the space agency of India. The department executes the Indian Space Programme primarily through various Centres or units within ISROISRO was previously the Indian National Committee for Space Research (INCOSPAR), set up by the Government of India in 1962, as envisioned by Dr. VikramA Sarabhai.
